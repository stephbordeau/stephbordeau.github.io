Licensed Massage and Bodywork Therapist (#17760), RYT-200
As a person with a nurturing and intuitive spirit, becoming a massage therapist was a natural progression in my journey. I have experienced the deep, profound healing that occurs through regular bodywork and wanted to be able to share this incredible healing gift with others. I am also a lifelong student of yoga and attribute my intuitive touch to years of work towards cultivating a deep awareness of Self. My approach to massage is to create a healing environment that is safe and nurturing, calming to the nervous system, and non judgmental, with intentions to allow pain to release from the body•mind•spirit. During a session with me, you can expect an integrative approach, blending different modalities such as Swedish, deep tissue, myofascial release and trigger point therapy to achieve your desired healing goals. I also love the healing properties of aromatherapy and sound healing and often incorporate these complimentary modalities into my sessions to create a deeper healing experience for you.
I am a 2018 Graduate from Gwinnett College graduating Summa Cum Laude. While attending school, I also completed my prenatal massage certification. I completed my 200-hr Registered Yoga Teacher Training in 2013 and later went on to obtain my certification to teach prenatal yoga.
I am honored to be a part of your healing journey and will welcome you with an open heart.

Services

  • The Arvigo Techniques of Maya Abdominal Therapy® are founded on an ancient Maya technique of abdominal massage. This technique is performed externally by gently guiding and supporting organs back into their optimal position, allowing for improved functioning for optimal health and wellness. Arvigo® Therapy is best known for addressing misalignment or congestion in the reproductive organs of both men and women, as well as many common digestive disorders. For more detailed information on The Arvigo Techniques of Maya Abdominal Therapy®, please visit: arvigotherapy.com

    The first Arvigo® Therapy session is a 2-hour session and includes a thorough client intake and health history review, a full treatment, and self-care instruction.

  • An integrative therapeutic massage blends modalities to create the best healing experience for you. To best achieve your goals, one or all of the following modalities may be used in your treatment session: Swedish, deep tissue, myofascial release and trigger point therapy. Aromatherapy and sound healing are also available to deepen your healing experience.

  • Swedish abdominal massage (60 min) is a beautiful way to clear held stress and anxiety that often gives us that uneasy feeling or "knot in the gut". This clearing of stagnant energy brings ease to emotions and is extremely beneficial to our overall digestive system. To amplify the detoxifying effects of the abdominal massage, a castor oil pack will be applied to the abdomen. As you rest and let the castor oil’s healing properties take affect, you will receive a nourishing head/face/neck and foot Swedish massage to further promote circulation and whole body relaxation. The abdominal massage session will conclude with a customized aromatherapy blend designed to calm the nervous system, support gut health, and align the 3rd Chakra.

  • Prenatal massage (60 min) is a relaxing and nurturing massage dedicated to relieving tension and pain the body experiences during pregnancy.
