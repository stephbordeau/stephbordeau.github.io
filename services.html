Arvigo® Therapy

The Arvigo Techniques of Maya Abdominal Therapy® are founded on an ancient Maya technique of abdominal massage. This technique is performed externally by gently guiding and supporting organs back into their optimal position, allowing for improved functioning for optimal health and wellness. Arvigo® Therapy is best known for addressing misalignment or congestion in the reproductive organs of both men and women, as well as many common digestive disorders.

Integrative Therapeutic Massage

An integrative therapeutic massage blends the modalities of Swedish, deep tissue, myofascial release, trigger point therapy, cupping therapy and aromatherapy to create the best healing experience for you.

Swedish

Swedish massage is used to relax the entire body. It is exceptionally beneficial for increasing the level of oxygen in the blood, decreasing muscle toxins, improving circulation and easing tension throughout the body.

Deep Tissue

Deep tissue massage is slow, deep and specific with the focus of accessing deeper layers of muscle tissue and connective tissue in order to release chronic muscle tension and pain.

Myofascial Release

Myofascial Release uses sustained gentle pressure to stretch and loosen constricted fascia. It eliminates pain by relaxing contracted muscles and restoring range of motion to restricted tissue.

Trigger Point Therapy

Trigger Point Therapy is specifically designed to alleviate the source of the pain through cycles of isolated pressure and release. A trigger point is a tight area or “knot” within muscle tissue that can cause both localized pain and pain in other parts of the body. In this type of massage, the recipient actively participates through deep breathing as well as identifying the exact location and intensity of the discomfort.
